Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

BoostKaro aapki privacy ko seriously leta hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum kaunsi information collect karte hain, use kaise karte hain, aur kaise protect karte hain jab aap hamari website aur services use karte hain.

2. Information We Collect

Jab aap order place karte hain, hum collect karte hain:

  • Email Address — order confirmation aur updates ke liye
  • Mobile Number — WhatsApp pe updates bhejne ke liye
  • Social Media Link — jis profile/video pe promotion karna hai
  • Payment Information — Razorpay ke through process hota hai (hum card details store nahi karte)

3. How We Use Your Information

Collected information ka use hota hai:

  • Order process karne aur deliver karne ke liye
  • Order confirmation aur campaign updates email/WhatsApp karne ke liye
  • Customer support provide karne ke liye
  • Service improve karne ke liye (anonymized data)

4. Data Sharing

Hum aapka data third parties ko sell nahi karte. Sirf in cases mein share hota hai:

  • Razorpay — payment processing ke liye
  • Google (Ads) — campaign delivery ke liye (sirf target URL)
  • Meta (Ads) — campaign delivery ke liye (sirf target URL)
  • Legal requirement — agar court order ya government request ho

5. Data Storage

  • Order data Google Sheets mein securely store hota hai.
  • Payment data Razorpay ke secure servers pe store hota hai — hum card/UPI details store nahi karte.
  • Data retention period: 2 saal tak orders ki information rakhte hain.

6. Cookies

Hamari website basic cookies use karti hai jo site ko properly function karne deti hain. Hum advertising cookies ya tracking cookies use nahi karte.

7. Your Rights

Aapke paas ye rights hain:

  • Apna stored data dekh sakte hain
  • Apna data delete karwa sakte hain
  • Marketing communications se opt-out kar sakte hain

In rights ke liye humse contact karein: alokkmohan@zohomail.in

8. Security

Hum aapki data protect karne ke liye industry-standard measures use karte hain. Lekin koi bhi online transmission 100% secure nahi hoti. Razorpay ke through payment karna safest option hai.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

Hum is policy ko update kar sakte hain. Changes website pe publish ki jaayengi. Major changes ke liye aapko email notification dene ki koshish karenge.
