Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

✅
Hamara Promise

Agar campaign start nahi ho paata, aapko full refund diya jaata hai — koi sawaal nahi.

1. Refund Eligible Cases

Aapko refund milega agar:

  • Campaign technical issues ki wajah se start hi nahi ho paata
  • Galat platform ya service deliver hoti hai (aapke order ke according nahi)
  • Payment successful hua lekin order system mein register nahi hua
  • Hum apni side se 72 hours mein campaign start karne mein fail ho jaate hain

2. Non-Refundable Cases

Refund nahi milega agar:

  • Campaign already shuru ho chuka hai (views/followers deliver hone lag gaye)
  • Aapne galat URL diya jis wajah se delivery nahi hui
  • Aapka account private tha jis wajah se promotion kaam nahi kiya
  • Platform (YouTube/Instagram/Facebook) ne aapka account suspend kar diya
  • Aapne khud account ya content delete kar diya campaign ke dauran
  • Mind change ya "mujhe nahi chahiye" — payment ke baad order cancel nahi hota

3. Partial Refund

Kuch cases mein partial refund diya ja sakta hai:

  • Agar promised quantity ka sirf kuch hissa deliver hua (e.g., 500 mein se 200 views mile)
  • Aise cases mein undelivered portion ka proportional refund diya jaata hai

4. How to Request Refund

Refund request karne ke liye:

  1. Email karein: alokkmohan@zohomail.in
  2. Subject: Refund Request — [Order ID]
  3. Apna Order ID, registered email, aur reason likho
  4. Hum 48 ghante mein reply karenge

5. Refund Processing Time

  • Refund approve hone ke baad Razorpay ke through process hota hai
  • Amount same source pe wapas aata hai (UPI → UPI, Card → Card)
  • Processing time: 5–7 business days
  • Bank holidays pe extra time lag sakta hai

6. Dispute Resolution

Agar aap refund decision se satisfied nahi hain:

  • Pehle humse directly email pe baat karein
  • Razorpay ke Dispute Resolution process bhi use kar sakte hain
  • Hum hamesha fair resolution dhundhne ki koshish karte hain

7. Contact for Refund

Refund ya kisi bhi issue ke liye:

📧 alokkmohan@zohomail.in

Response time: 24–48 hours (working days)
