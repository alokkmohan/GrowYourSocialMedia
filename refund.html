⚠️ Campaign Fail / Reject Hone Par — Full Refund

Kabhi-kabhi Google Ads ya Meta (Facebook/Instagram) kisi content ko approve nahi karte — ye platform ka apna decision hota hai aur hum isko control nahi kar sakte. Aisi situation mein aapka poora paisa (100%) wapas aapke original payment source par return kiya jaata hai. Aapko kuch karne ki zaroorat nahi — hum khud 48 ghante mein refund initiate kar dete hain aur aapko email/WhatsApp par inform karte hain.

1. Refund Eligible Cases

Aapko refund milega agar:

  • Google Ads ya Meta ne campaign approve nahi kiya (ad rejection) — 100% full refund
  • Campaign technical issues ki wajah se start hi nahi ho paata — 100% full refund
  • Galat platform ya service deliver hoti hai (aapke order ke according nahi)
  • Payment successful hua lekin order system mein register nahi hua
  • Hum apni side se 48 hours mein campaign start karne mein fail ho jaate hain

2. Non-Refundable Cases

Refund nahi milega agar:

  • Campaign already shuru ho chuka hai (views/followers deliver hone lag gaye)
  • Aapne galat URL diya jis wajah se delivery nahi hui
  • Aapka account private tha jis wajah se promotion kaam nahi kiya
  • Platform (YouTube/Instagram/Facebook) ne aapka account suspend kar diya
  • Aapne khud account ya content delete kar diya campaign ke dauran
  • Mind change ya "mujhe nahi chahiye" — payment ke baad order cancel nahi hota

3. Partial Refund

Kuch cases mein partial refund diya ja sakta hai:

  • Agar promised quantity ka sirf kuch hissa deliver hua (e.g., 500 mein se 200 views mile)
  • Aise cases mein undelivered portion ka proportional refund diya jaata hai
