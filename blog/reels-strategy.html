Reels Strategy: 0 Se 1 Lakh Views Kaise Paaye

👤 Alok Mohan📅 April 2026⏱ 7 min read
🎬

Reels aaj ke time mein sabse fastest growing content format hai Instagram aur Facebook dono pe. Sahi strategy se ek video lakhs views tak pahonch sakta hai.

Hook — Pehle 2 Second Sabse Important

Pehle 2 second mein viewer nahi ruka to wo scroll kar dega. Strong hooks:

  • "Ye jaanna chahte ho?" — curiosity
  • "Maine ye galti ki — aap mat karna" — relatability
  • "Sirf 30 second mein seekho" — time value
  • Shocking visual ya unexpected moment

Winning Reel Ka Structure

  1. 0-2 sec: Hook — roka raho
  2. 2-20 sec: Content — value do
  3. Last 3 sec: CTA — "Follow karo tips ke liye"

Trending Audio Ka Fayda Uthao

Trending audio pe banaye Reels ko platform extra push deta hai. Explore karke dekho jo audio baar baar aaye — wo use karo apne content pe.

Caption Mein Value Do

Sirf "check this out" mat likho. Ek mini tip ya story likho jo video se related ho. Isse save aur shares badhte hain.

Thumbnail Attractive Banao

Bold text + clear visual = zyada clicks. Canva se free mein professional cover bana sakte ho.

💡 Viral Formula: Entertaining + Educational + Relatable = Viral Reel. Teen mein se do bhi ho to achha perform karega.

Post Karne Ke Baad

Pehle ghante mein:

  • Story mein share karo
  • Comments ka reply karo turant
  • Apne niche ke 5-10 accounts pe engage karo

Initial Views Kyun Zaroori Hain?

Jab Reel pe views aate hain — chahe organic ho ya paid — Instagram use Explore page pe push karta hai. Wahan se organic reach exponentially badhti hai.
